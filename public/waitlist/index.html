For Players

Everything you need to perform at your best.

Core Feature

AI Programme Builder

Input your position, playing style, age, height, weight, gym access, and season phase. The AI instantly generates a fully personalised multi-week training programme with no generic plans.

Match-Day Periodisation

Your programme automatically adjusts around matchday. Heavier loads early in the week taper into the game so you train hard without compromising your performance when it matters most.

Active Workout Mode

A real-time session tracker that guides you through each exercise, logs your weights and reps, tracks rest periods, and saves every session automatically on completion.

ACWR Load Calendar

Powered by the Acute:Chronic Workload Ratio methodology used by professional clubs. Visualise safe zones and high-risk zones week by week so you never overtrain or break down.

Fitness Testing Battery

Six professional-grade tests: 10m Sprint, 30m Sprint, Countermovement Jump, Standing Long Jump, Repeated Sprint Ability, and Yo-Yo Test. Results are graded and fed directly back into your programme.

Training History and Progress

A full searchable log of every completed session. Track your personal bests, monitor improvement over time, and review previous workouts in detail.

Exercise Library

Browse every exercise used across all programmes, complete with descriptions and coaching cues. Tap any exercise to understand exactly how to perform it correctly.

Referral System

Share your personal referral code and give friends 21 extra days of free Premium. You earn additional free days for every player you bring onto the platform.

For Coaches

Manage your squad. Monitor every player.

Coach Pro

Squad Dashboard and Readiness Heatmap

See your entire squad at a glance. A colour-coded readiness heatmap shows every player's status: Ready, Moderate, or Low, so you know exactly who is available before matchday.

Schedule and Phase Planning

Set the current training phase and plan the week. Every player's individual programme auto-periodises around your squad schedule so the whole group peaks together.

Fitness Test Leaderboards

Ranked leaderboards for all six fitness tests across your squad. Identify your fastest and most powerful players, and export all test data as a CSV with one tap.

Compliance and Attendance Tracking

Monitor session completion rates across the whole squad. An activity feed shows recent player actions: sessions completed, tests recorded, and new players joining.

Player Detail View and Private Notes

Tap any player to see their current programme, all six test results, improvement trends, and recent activity. Add private coach notes that only you can see.

Squad Invite System

Every coach gets a unique invite code. Players enter it on sign-up and are instantly linked to your squad. Pro coaches automatically unlock full Premium access for every player in the team.
