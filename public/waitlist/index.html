For Players

Everything you need to perform at your best.

Most training apps treat football as an afterthought. With Vector, the match schedule is the foundation and everything else fits around it.

Core feature
AI Programme Builder

Position, play style, gym access, season phase. The AI generates a fully personalised multi-week programme instantly. No generic plans.

Periodised to your fixture list

Sessions shift based on how many days you are from a match. Heavy loads early in the week taper into gameday so you train hard without compromising performance.

Train to your actual numbers

10m, 30m, CMJ, RSA, Yo-Yo. Run the testing battery and Vector sets loads from your real scores, not a percentage of some average player's numbers.

Works with what you have got

Full gym, basic gym, or just a park. Tell it your access and the programme adapts. Change it any time. Your plan updates for the new situation.

Active workout mode

A real-time session tracker guides you through each exercise, logs your weights and reps, tracks rest periods, and saves every session automatically.

Progressive overload, done for you

Sets, reps, intensity, and rest periods progress across weeks automatically. You do not need to plan it. Just show up and do the session.

For Coaches

Manage your squad. Monitor every player.

See your whole squad at a glance. Know who is ready before matchday. Every player's programme auto-periodises around your schedule.

Coach Pro
Squad readiness heatmap

A colour-coded heatmap shows every player's status: Ready, Moderate, or Low. Know exactly who is available before matchday without asking anyone.

Fitness test leaderboards

Ranked leaderboards for all six fitness tests across your squad. Identify your fastest and most powerful players and export all data as a CSV with one tap.

Compliance tracking

Monitor session completion rates across the whole squad. An activity feed shows recent player actions: sessions completed, tests recorded, and new players joining.

Squad invite system

Every coach gets a unique invite code. Players enter it on sign-up and are instantly linked to your squad. Pro coaches unlock Premium access for every player in the team.
