Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

Vector Football ("we", "us", "our") operates the Vector Football waitlist website and mobile application. Our contact email is vectorfootball.com@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

When you sign up to our waitlist we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time of your signup

When you use the Vector Football app we may also collect your name, position, age, height, weight, training data, and fitness test results for the purpose of generating your personalised training programme.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your email address to:

  • Send you early access updates about Vector Football
  • Notify you when the app is available
  • Send product news and updates you have consented to receive

We will never sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Waitlist email addresses are stored securely via Mailchimp. App data is stored via Supabase. Both services are GDPR compliant.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at vectorfootball.com@gmail.com. You can also unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

6. Cookies

Our waitlist site uses minimal cookies for analytics purposes only. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at vectorfootball.com@gmail.com.
