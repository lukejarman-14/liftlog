1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.

  • Functional cookies to remember form state during your session

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our site may set cookies from the following third-party services:

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the site. For guidance on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.
