Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2025  ·  GDPR compliant

Vector Football ("we", "us") is a strength & conditioning app for footballers. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights under UK and EU data protection law (UK GDPR / GDPR).

1. Who we are

Vector Football is operated as an independent app. If you have any questions about this policy or want to exercise your rights, email us at privacy@vectorfootball.co.uk.

2. What data we collect

Category Examples Why
Account data Name, email address, hashed password To create and secure your account
Profile data Playing position, experience level, height, weight To personalise your training programme
Training data Workout sessions, sets, reps, weights, RPE scores To track progress and adapt your programme
Health & fitness data Sprint times, jump height, Yo-Yo test results, daily readiness scores, body weight log To generate fitness baselines and show progression
Match schedule data Match dates, opponent names, session intensity ratings To periodise training load around your fixtures
Usage data Screens visited, features used, events fired (anonymised) To improve the app (via PostHog analytics)
Payment data Subscription status, transaction ID To manage your premium subscription

3. Legal basis for processing

  • Contract — processing your account and training data to provide the service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — anonymised usage analytics to improve the app.
  • Legal obligation — retaining transaction records as required by UK tax law.

4. Third-party services

Supabase

Cloud database and authentication. Your account data and training history are stored on Supabase servers in the EU. Supabase Privacy Policy →

RevenueCat

Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat processes your subscription status and transaction IDs. RevenueCat Privacy Policy →

PostHog

Product analytics. We send anonymised event data (no name or email) to PostHog to understand feature usage. You can opt out via the Profile screen. PostHog Privacy Policy →

Stripe

Payment processing for web subscriptions. Stripe handles card details directly — we never see or store your card number. Stripe Privacy Policy →

5. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (Profile → Delete Account & All Data), all personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

6. Your rights (UK GDPR / GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data (Profile → Export my data gives you a full JSON export instantly)
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data via the Profile screen
  • Erasure — delete your account and all data (Profile → Delete Account & All Data)
  • Portability — export your data in machine-readable format (JSON export in Profile)
  • Object — opt out of analytics tracking at any time via the Profile screen
  • Restriction — request we restrict processing of your data

To exercise any right not covered by in-app features, email privacy@vectorfootball.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you are based in the UK.

7. Children

Vector Football is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are never stored in plaintext — they are hashed using SHA-256 locally and managed by Supabase Auth. We use industry-standard security practices and review them regularly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Significant changes will be notified in-app.
